Help Centre

Find instant solutions for vehicles, operations, and login issues.

Where is my assigned vehicle?

Go to the Assigned Vehicle page in the main menu to see the car currently assigned to you by the admin team.

How do I report a mechanical issue?

On your vehicle details page, click Report Issue to send a maintenance request to the company.

How do I accept an assignment?

When an admin assigns a vehicle, it will appear on your Home screen. You must click Accept to confirm your shift.

What if I don't like the assigned car?

If the vehicle is not suitable, you can click Request Change or Decline to notify the admin.

How do I reset my password?

On the Sign-In page, click Forgot Password. Enter your email to receive a link to reset your credentials.

My account is locked. What now?

Accounts are temporarily locked after 5 failed login attempts. Please wait 15 minutes or contact dispatch for a manual reset.

The dashboard is not loading properly.

Ensure you have a stable internet connection and try refreshing the browser. If the video background lags, check your device performance settings.
